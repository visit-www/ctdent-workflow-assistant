Windows desktop companion · v2.1.3

Your case list, as a live one-click worklist

CT Dent Assistant runs alongside the CT Dent reporting portal. For any case it signs you in, downloads the scan in your preferred format, and launches the viewer you chose — while logging every case opened for easy monthly reporting.

⬇ Download for Windows
CT_Dent_Setup.exe ~60 MB Windows 10 / 11 · 64-bit

What to expect

  • No admin rights needed. It installs just for you — no UAC prompt.
  • No browser to install. It uses the Edge, Chrome or Brave you already have.
  • Setup takes about two minutes, once. After that it reconnects on its own.

Windows will warn you that the installer is unsigned. That is expected — choose More info → Run anyway. A code-signing certificate is on the roadmap.

What's new v2.1.3

  • Sign-in works. The portal's picture check will not accept a page that an app is touching — so the app was breaking your own login. It now keeps its hands off the page entirely while you sign in, and picks up the session once you reach your worklist.
  • No password box. The app never asks for, types or stores your portal login. You sign in yourself the first time; after that it remembers the session, and later launches open your worklist with no sign-in at all.
  • You choose your DICOM viewer. The app finds the viewers installed on your PC and asks which to use — RadiAnt, Carestream, anything. No hardcoded path, and if a viewer is later moved or removed it tells you and offers to re-pick it.
  • Nothing lands in a folder you didn't pick. On first run you choose where scans are stored. There is no default location.

Requirements

The app bundles neither a browser nor a viewer — it drives the ones you already have.

  • Windows 10 or 11 (64-bit)
  • Microsoft Edge, Google Chrome, or Brave
  • A CT Dent portal account, and access to wherever your one-time code arrives
  • A DICOM viewer — whichever you prefer, if you work in DICOM
  • A secure folder for downloaded scans — they are patient records

Setting up

Once, and you are done.

  1. Run the installer. It installs for your user only, so there is no admin prompt.
  2. Choose where scans are stored. Pick a secure, access-controlled folder. Nothing downloads until you do.
  3. Pick your browser and preferred format. Choosing DICOM asks which installed viewer to open scans with; Volume Viewer downloads bring their own.
  4. Click Start Session and sign in yourself, in the browser window that opens — password, picture check and one-time code. The app deliberately stays off the page while you do. You should only need to do that once: it remembers the session, and later launches open your worklist straight away.
